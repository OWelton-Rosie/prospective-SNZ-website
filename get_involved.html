As a completely volunteer-run organisation, Speedcubing New Zealand relies on volunteers to run competitions, manage our social media presence and make major decisions. We always need new volunteers to fill a variety of roles! You can view our current vacancies below.

Volunteering opportunities

As per our About page, SNZ's stff are split into 3 different groups: WCA Delegates, SNZ Promotional Team and SNZ Governance. For more information on their roles, click here.

In addition to SNZ staff, we always need people to perform staffing duties at competitions, including but not limited to running, judging and scrambling duties. If you are a trusted person (as decided by the competition's WCA Delegates), you may be encouraged to do data entry (WCA Live) duties.

Current vacancies

Currently, there are no listed vacancies. If you think you have the skills and credentials required, contact us.
